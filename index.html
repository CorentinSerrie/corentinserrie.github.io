Background

I am a graduate from Centrale Paris in france, and from the DTU in Copenhagen, where I specialized in video games, and wrote my thesis on Open Development in video games production.

I started really getting into game development in my gap year in 2017, when after 6 month developing prototypes for various companies with other students of the Digital Tech Year, I joined Ubisoft Montreal for an internship. This was a really good oportunity to get insight into this industry, and got me craving for more. I then integrated DTU in the hope of joining the DADIU program, which I did in fall 2018 as a lead programmer. This was an amazing experience, where I honed the leadership skills taught to us at Centrale. It cemented my will to work in this industry, preferably with small teams iterating quickly on interesting gameplay.

I have since joined Lightbulb Crew, where I've been working on Nova Hearts for the past years. There, I experienced all the steps of a professional production, and grew an interest in developping tools and automating processes to facilitate the work of my team. I became responsible of our CI/CD platform, improving it along the way, and it is now a valuable asset to the company.
